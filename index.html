About Me

Google Scholar | CV

I am a Machine Learning Ph.D. student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech. My advisor is Zsolt Kira, who leads the RobotIcs Perception and Learning (RIPL) lab. My research vision is to build lifelong machine learning models which protect data privacy and reduce compute. To work towards my goals, I work on problems such as: lifelong/continual learning, knowledge distillation, federated learning, and learning from limited labels.
